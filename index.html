Welcome to Arunodaya Public School

Arunodaya Public School was established in 1996 under the aegis of Arunodaya Education Society, New Delhi. It started as a primary school and has now blossomed into a senior secondary school affiliated to CBSE. The school embarks on the mission of providing good education and creating individuals who are confident their potential, diligent to work towards their goal and above all co-creators of their own destiny. At APS, it is the child centered education which aims at: All round Personality Development centered around character building. Value- oriented teaching.
